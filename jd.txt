Who Are We Looking For



We look for big thinkers. People who can drive positive change, step up and show what’s next – people with passion, can-do attitude and a hunger to learn and grow. In practice this means:
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).
Strong programming skills (e.g., Java, Python, or JavaScript) and experience with at least one automation framework (e.g., Robot, Selenium, Playwright, Cypress, Appium).
Solid experience with API testing (RESTful/SOAP) and tools (Postman, REST Assured, Newman, etc.).
Experience building and maintaining test automation frameworks, data-driven testing, and test utilities.
Familiarity with CI/CD tools (Jenkins, GitHub Actions, GitLab CI) and version control (Git).
Understanding of software development life cycle and agile methodologies.
Strong problem-solving, debugging, and analytical skills.
Excellent communication and collaboration abilities.
5 years of experience automation systems and related infrastructure.
3 years of experience in testing, maintaining, or launching software products.
Understanding of payment industry business and operations.
Previous experience as a Full Stack SW Engineer is a plus.
